## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

What went well was that our team was able to talk through different ideas and eventually choose a project direction that fits the course requirements well. Once we decided on e-commerce, it became easier to come up with user roles, user stories, and features. We also made good progress by dividing up the work and helping each other where needed.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

One challenge was trying not to make the project too big. There are a lot of things you can add to an e-commerce app, so we had to think carefully about what made sense for this project and what should stay simple for now. That is also why we decided not to overcomplicate things by adding Sellers as a user role at this stage. Another challenge was getting comfortable with the shared GitHub workflow while working in the same repository.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?

Going forward, we will need support with designing the database, planning relationships between tables, and organizing the app structure on both the frontend and backend. We will also need to make sure we stay on track with the required features and do not try to implement too much at once.
